Rules
I. Darts Each participant will be given 4 darts he has to score the max points in this game. (PLAYER VS PLAYER)
II. Ring Toss
In this game there will certain objects fixed in particular places the participant will be given certain rings he has to toss the rings to the fixed objects. (TEAM VS TEAM)
III. Fish Cup
In this game there will be certain number of cups placed on the table filled with water & certain cups will have a toy the participant has to throw the ping pong ball into the cups which has the toy. (SINGLE)
IV. Knock Down
There will certain placed on the table the participant has to hit the target. (TEAM VS TEAM)
V. Bean Game
In this game there will be beans kept in one cup. The participant needs to pass the beans into the other cup. (TEAM VS TEAM)
VI. Penny Drop
In this game there will be a transparent glass kept in which there will be a circle made. The participant needs to throw the penny into the circle made (would be given certain number of pennies). (SINGLE)
VII. MATCH UP
In this game the participants will be made a team of 2, they will be given with certain number of balls. The participants need to match up there timings and throw the ball. (one person needs to throw a ball & the other has to tap the ball into the bin)
VIII. PASS THE BALL
In this game the participants will have to make a team of 5, they need pass the ball using exam boards & the 5th person standing has to pass the ball into the bin.

Project Exhibition Rules and Regulations
Introduction:
The JCET organizing " Project Exhibition" as part of Vaibhav-2019. Project Exhibition helps to students enhancing their knowledge and skills. The JCET has initiated this innovative platform to promote science culture in the society.
Objective:
The "Project Exhibition" is conducted with the below mentioned themes.
• Environmental issues: causes and consequences of climatic change;
• Biodiversity: Conservation and Sustenance
• Life science in human welfare;
• Alternative energy (green energy or bio-fuel) as a substitute of hydrocarbon energy;
• Information and Communication Technology
• Mathematics-physical science
Rules and Regulations:
• All candidates interested in taking part in the event should fill registration form.
• In a team maximum three members are allowed.
• After selecting topic for their project, they should submit the chart/poster regarding their project to event co-ordinators.
• The chart should contain just a simple description about their project. The candidates can take help of following points to describe their project in the chart.
 Working diagram of model, which can be hand drawn or a printed one.
 Introduction
 Block diagram
 Working principle
 Advantage and disadvantages and future scope
• Basic requirement i.e. electric power supply of 230V AC will be made available to participants as per requirement of project.
• Participants should bring all their requirements & all group members are responsible their projects.
• Breaking the rules by any one member from group is considered as hole group is disqualified in any stage.
• The decision of the judges shall be treated as final and binding & the organizers reserve the rights to change above rules.
